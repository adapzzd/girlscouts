Girl Scouts: Geocaching Badge

Find out about geocaching. You'll search for treasure chests, called "geocaches," using a device called a GPS receiver. When you've earned this badge, you'll know how to have a geocaching adventure.

1. Prepare for your adventure

2. Learn to use a GPS receiver

3. Make a trade item

4. Go on a geocaching adventure

5. Take part in a bug's travels!
