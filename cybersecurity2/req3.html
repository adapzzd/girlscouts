What Information Should I Not Share?

Here are some examples of rules you should follow when sharing online:

  • Never post any personal information.
  • Always reread what you write before you post it. Things that you post last forever online.
  • Always make your posts short and easy to understand/
  • Never use all caps (unless you really mean it). All caps is usually interpereted as screaming. I reccomend not using caps unles it is a positive message (like 'That video is AWESOME!!!').
  • Always think before friending someone online because all you know about them is what they tell you (It's probably a good idea to only friend people that you know).
  • Never impersonate other people online.
  • Always think about who sees your message when posting in a group chat or sending a message to multiple people.
