Albequerque Zoo

During my vacation in Albequerque, New Mexico, my family and I went to the Albequerque BioPark Zoo. They had a couple signs about wolves there, and because I was making a pamphlet about wolves, I thought it would be a good idea to learn about them. The wolves I was reading about were in the zoo for a breeding program, so the viewing windovs were small and the observers were instructed to try to be quiet around the wolves.
