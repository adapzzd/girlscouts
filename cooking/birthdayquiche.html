Birthday Quiche

Filling Recipe:

  • 6 slices bacon and 2 slices ham
  • 1 medium onion, sliced and cooked
  • as much spinach as you want
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk (I used cream, water, and yogurt)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • dash of ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1 tbs of all-purpose flour

Crust Recipe:

  • 1 3/4 cups of flour (preferably half spelt half white)
  • 1/3 cups butter
  • 4 - 5 tbs cold water
